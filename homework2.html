MO:MO

Mo:Mo: is very popular dish of Nepal. It is Nepali mandu. It is said to be originated from Tibet but later was popularized by Newari community from Kathmandu Valley. This dish can be said one of the favorite dish of Nepali people.

How Mo:Mo: is made?

There are many ways of making this dish. Every Nepali has thier own way of making Mo:Mo:. And my family has also its own way to make. While preparing this dish, we need dough which should be very soft and smooth so it does not brust or have holes while rolling it into roti or adding the fillings. Fillings could be of anything. We can make of meat or vegetables only. But my personal favorite is of pork. But we can add any meat fillings like chicxken, buffalo's meat or beef.To making filling we need add mo:mo spices, ginger, onion and parsley. But we can add more ingridients according to our likings.

My Family's way of making MO:MO:

First, take flour as much as we need for 3-4 people and make a dough which should be smooth. Then leave it for 2-3 hours inside the fridge wrapped in plastic. Between that time we need to make filling. We are going to make pork's momo so we take minced meat enough for 3-4 people. We put 4 table spoon of momo masala, one table spoon of ginger, add four finely chopped onions, half finely chopped cabbages and mixed it well. Add salt according to your taste. We can add MSG but it is not compulsary. We take a pan and heat 6 table spoons of oil and add it to the filling. Then mix it well. After fillling is ready, its time to roll the dough and put filling inside it by making beautiful design. The most challenging work while making momo is making design.

List of Ingredients required for making mo:mo: for 3-4 people:
  1. 1/2 kilo of flour
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. 200 grams of minced pork
  4. 4 onions which is finely chopped
  5. half of the cabbage which is also finely chopped
  6. one table spoon of ginger
  7. 4 table spoon of MO:MO masala
  8. Salt and MSG according to our taste
  9. 6 table spoon of oil
